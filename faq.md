# FAQ

## Is ZaminShop free?
Depends on the release model.

## Does it support PlaceholderAPI?
Yes.

## Does it support Folia?
The architecture is designed with modern threading concerns in mind.

## Can I create custom GUIs?
Yes. Most menus are YAML-driven.

## Is search supported?
Yes — search is a major feature.

## Can I migrate from ShopGUI+?
Mostly yes, though formats may differ.

## Does it support 1.8?
Compatibility layers exist for older versions.
